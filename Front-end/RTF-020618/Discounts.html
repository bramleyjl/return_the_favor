The Discount Program

A Special THANKS to our Community of Businesses who honor our Veterans by participating in the F.A.V.O.R. Veterans Discount Program. Honorably discharged Veterans file their original discharge papers with their local County Clerks Office and receive a personalized F.A.V.O.R. ID card that they can present to participating local businesses. Veterans should contact their local County Clerks for more info about Filing their original DD-214 paperwork

Veterans Subscribe to our Newsletter

Veterans should subscribe to our newsletter to be notified about the latest Discounts and Events. All fields are required
