  • About

Thank you for visiting ReturnTheFAVOR.vet. I started this website because I noticed the Return the FAVOR participating Business List was out of date and difficult to use. Initially I just wanted to put my county’s vendor list online so it could be accessible from any smartphone, but as I researched the Return The FAVOR program and talked with other veterans, I discovered a bigger opportunity to assist my fellow veterans of New York State.

The ReturnTheFAVOR.vet mission is to create a sense of an online community for veterans by linking them to resources, events, and businesses in their region and across the United States.

ReturnTheFAVOR.vet will do this by:

  • Informing veterans of businesses that are happy to give discounts on products and services.
  • Informing veterans on where to go to register for the Return The FAVOR program in their county.
  • Linking veterans with County, State and Federal veteran programs and information.
  • Linking veterans with Non-Government Offices (NGOs) who want to assist and aid veterans.
  • Promoting, advertising and marketing veteran organizations, events, veteran friendly businesses and veteran needs.

–Jason Lebrecht
Service-Disabled Army Veteran
